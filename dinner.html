Dinner Recipes

All recipes here were made by Morgan, Luiz, Ryan and Gabe

Baked Salmon With Lemon-Dill Sauce

Ingredients

  • salmon filets
  • Lemon
  • Dill
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil

Instructions

Season salmon with lemon, dill, and garlic. Bake until flaky.


Vegetarian Chickpea Curry

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Curry Spices

Instructions

Cook chickpeas with tomatoes, onions, garlic, and curry spices. Serve over rice.


Beef Chicken Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • Beef strips
  • broccoli
  • carrots
  • soy sauce
  • ginger

Instructions

Stir-fry beef and veggies in soy sauce and ginger. Serve over noodles.


Stuffed Bell Peppers with Ground Turkey

Ingredients

  • Bell peppers
  • ground turkey
  • quinoa
  • black beans
  • tomatoes

Instructions

Stuff peppers with a mixture of turkey, quinoa, beans, and tomatoes. Bake until peppers are tender.


Lemon Herb Grilled Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • lemon
  • quinoa
  • herbs (rosemary, thyme)
  • olive oil

Instructions

Marinate chicken in lemon, herbs, and oil. Grill until cooked through.
