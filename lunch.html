Lunch Time Recipes

All recipes here were made by Morgan, Luiz and Ryan

Chicken Caesar Salad Wrap

Igredients

  • Grilled chicken
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Caesar dressing
  • Whole wheat tortilla

Instructions

  1. Fill tortilla with chicken, lettuce, and dressing.
  2. Roll up and serve.

Quinoa and Chickpea Salad

Ingredients

  • Cooked quinoa
  • Chickpeas
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Feta cheese
  • Lemon vinaigrette

Instructions

  1. Toss ingredients together
  2. Drizzle with vinaigrette

Turkey and Avocado Wrap

Ingredients

  • Sliced turkey, avocado, lettuce, tomato
  • Whole grain wrap

Instructions

  1. Layer ingredients on the wrap
  2. Roll and enjoy

Caprese Panini

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Whole grain bread
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions

  1. Assemble sandwich with mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil
  2. Grill until cheese melts
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze

Vegetarian Buddha Bowl

Ingredients

  • Quinoa
  • Roasted sweet potatoes
  • Chickpeas
  • Avocado
  • Kale
  • Tahini dressing

Instructions

  1. Arrange ingredients in a bowl
  2. Drizzle with tahini dressing

Mediterranean Pasta Salad

Ingredients

  • Pasta

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Toss with veggies, feta, and dressing

Tuna Salad Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Celery
  • Red onion
  • Mayo
  • Lettuce leaves

Instructions

  1. Mix tuna, celery, onion, and mayo
  2. Spoon into lettuce leaves
