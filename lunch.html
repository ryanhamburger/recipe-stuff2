Lunch Time Recipes

All recipes here were made by Morgan, Luiz, Ryan and Gabe

Chicken Caesar Salad Wrap

Ingredients

  • Grilled chicken
  • Romaine lettuce
  • Caesar dressing
  • Whole wheat tortilla

Instructions

  1. Fill tortilla with chicken, lettuce, and dressing.
  2. Roll up and serve.

Quinoa and Chickpea Salad

Ingredients

  • Cooked quinoa
  • Chickpeas
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Feta cheese
  • Lemon vinaigrette

Instructions

  1. Toss ingredients together
  2. Drizzle with vinaigrette

Turkey and Avocado Wrap

Ingredients

  • Sliced turkey, avocado, lettuce, tomato
  • Whole grain wrap

Instructions

  1. Layer ingredients on the wrap
  2. Roll and enjoy

Caprese Panini

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Whole grain bread
  • Balsamic glaze

Instructions

  1. Assemble sandwich with mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil
  2. Grill until cheese melts
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze

Vegetarian Buddha Bowl

Ingredients

  • Quinoa

Instructions

  1. Arrange ingredients in a bowl
  2. Drizzle with tahini dressing

Veggie Quesadillas

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • Black beans

  • Bell peppers

Instructions

  2. Cook until cheese melts

Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Spinach
  • Feta cheese
  • Garlic
  • Lemon
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Mix spinach, feta, garlic, and lemon
  2. Stuff into chicken breast
  3. Bake until cooked
