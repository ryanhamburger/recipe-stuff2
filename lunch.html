Lunch Time Recipes

All recipes here were made by Morgan, Luiz, Ryan and Gabe

  • Chicken Caesar Salad Wrap

    Igredients

    • Grilled chicken
    • Romaine lettuce
    • Caesar dressing
    • Whole wheat tortilla

    Instructions

    1. Fill tortilla with chicken, lettuce, and dressing.
    2. Roll up and serve.

  • Quinoa and Chickpea Salad

    Ingredients

    • Cooked quinoa
    • Chickpeas
    • Cucumber
    • Cherry tomatoes
    • Feta cheese
    • Lemon vinaigrette

    Instructions

    1. Toss ingredients together
    2. Drizzle with vinaigrette

  • Turkey and Avocado Wrap

    Ingredients

    • Sliced turkey, avocado, lettuce, tomato
    • Whole grain wrap

    Instructions

    1. Layer ingredients on the wrap
    2. Roll and enjoy

  • Caprese Panini

    Ingredients

    • Mozzarella
    • Tomatoes
    • Fresh basil
    • Whole grain bread
    • Balsamic glaze

    Instructions

    1. Assemble sandwich with mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil
    2. Grill until cheese melts
    3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze

  • Vegetarian Buddha Bowl

    Ingredients

    • Quinoa
    • Roasted sweet potatoes
    • Chickpeas
    • Avocado
    • Kale
    • Tahini dressing

    Instructions

    1. Arrange ingredients in a bowl
    2. Drizzle with tahini dressing

  • Mediterranean Pasta Salad

    Ingredients

    Instructions

    1. Cook pasta

  • Tuna Salad Lettuce Wraps

    Ingredients

    • Tuna
    • Celery
    • Red onion
    • Mayo
    • Lettuce leaves

    Instructions

    1. Mix tuna, celery, onion, and mayo
    2. Spoon into lettuce leaves

  • Veggie Quesadillas

    Ingredients

    • Flour tortillas
    • Black beans
    • Corn
    • Bell peppers
    • Cheese
    • Salsa

    Instructions

    1. Fill tortillas with beans, corn, peppers, and cheese
    2. Cook until cheese melts
    3. Serve with salsa

    Instructions

  • Spinach and Feta Stuffed Chicken Breast

    Ingredients

    • Chicken breast
    • Spinach
    • Feta cheese
    • Garlic
    • Lemon
    • Olive oil

    Instructions

    1. Mix spinach, feta, garlic, and lemon
    2. Stuff into chicken breast
    3. Bake until cooked
